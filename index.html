About the Benchmark

This benchmark aims to provide an objective look at how modern LLMs perform on "real-world" tricky questions. Unlike standard benchmarks that might be present in training data, these questions are curated to test specific capabilities.

Methodology

  • Automated Grading: Consistent evaluation using script-based verification.
  • Cost Tracking: Monitor input/output tokens and associated costs.
  • Reproducibility: All questions and verifiers are open for inspection.
